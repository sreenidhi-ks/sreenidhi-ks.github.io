## Dr. Sreenidhi K. S.

Postdoctoral Fellow (Alexander von Humboldt Fellow), GFZ Potsdam  
PhD in Geodynamics – Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, India & Monash University, Australia  

Researcher in Computational Geodynamics working on plume–lithosphere interactions, rift dynamics, and the mechanical behaviour of the lithosphere.

I have several years of research experience in geophysics and geodynamics, combining numerical modelling, potential field methods, and data-driven approaches to investigate lithospheric structure and deformation in mantle plume–influenced and extensional tectonic settings — from continental rifts to mature mid-ocean ridges and passive margins.

I have worked in international collaborations with experts in geodynamics, structural geology, and geophysics, and I am interested in interdisciplinary research at the interface of geophysics, tectonics, and computational Earth science.
