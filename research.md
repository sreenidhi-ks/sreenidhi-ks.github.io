## Research Interests

My research focuses on understanding the dynamics and mechanical behaviour of the lithosphere in plume-influenced and extensional tectonic environments.

### Plume–Lithosphere Interaction
Investigating how mantle plumes interact with the lithosphere and influence deformation, magmatism, and rifting processes.

### Rift Dynamics
Studying the development and evolution of continental rifts and mid-ocean ridges using numerical modelling and geophysical observations.

### Lithospheric Mechanics
Exploring the strength and mechanical behaviour of the lithosphere, including the role of elastic thickness and structural inheritance.

### Computational Geodynamics
Using numerical modelling, machine learning, and data-driven approaches to study mantle dynamics and lithospheric deformation.
